Safety

In synthetic biology, especially when working on a project like ours - CalciCapture- with a novel organism such as Emiliania huxleyi, adhering to biosafety guidelines is extremely crucial. Our team at IIT Bombay places a strong emphasis on biosafety, fully understanding the potential risks of not following these protocols.

Initially, we considered utilizing a viral agent to enhance the calcification process in E. huxleyi, inspired by natural marine ecosystems. However, this approach raised significant biosafety and biosecurity concerns. Introducing such a solution could disrupt marine ecosystems and negatively impact native species.

Our team's Faculty Advisor Dr. Kiran Kondabagil also had experience in this domain due to him being a representative in the Institutional Biosafety Committee. This helped our team understand where potential safety concerns could arise in our solution and how we could handle them responsibly.
